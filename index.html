history

stadium

  • Beginning capacity of 50,000
  • New stadium opened in 1910
  • WW2 air raids, left the stadium unusable for 8 years
  • Repaired in 1949 & saw the club return to an improved stadium
  • Hosted 3 group matches of 1966 World Cup

ownership

  • Sponsored by the Lancashire & Yorkshire Railway company in 1982
  • December 1931, a £2000 investment saved Man Utd from bankruptcy
  • 1951 saw the Glazer family keep possession of Man Utd
  • 1971, Man Utd raised £6.7 million prior to a Sky UK takeover bid in 1998
  • January 2010, Man Utd were £716 million in debt

munich disaster

  • 1958 European Cup tie featured Man Utd travelling to Yugoslavia
